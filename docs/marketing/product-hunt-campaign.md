Hey PH! 👋

I built CVERiskPilot because I spent years in vulnerability management watching teams drown in thousands of CVEs with no way to prioritize.

The problem: Your scanner finds 8,000 vulnerabilities. Maybe 50 actually matter for your environment. But triaging manually takes forever, and most tools just sort by CVSS score (which misses real-world exploitation risk).

What we do differently:
• AI triage using EPSS (exploitation probability), KEV (known exploited), and your specific environment context
• The AI shows its work — you see which tools it called, what data it gathered, and its confidence level
• 10 compliance frameworks mapped automatically — no more spreadsheet hell
• Works with scanners you already use (Nessus, Qualys, Trivy, Snyk, etc.)
